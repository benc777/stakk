About Stakk Ventures

Stakk Ventures is a venture partnership based in Toronto. We specialize in building and investing in innovative B2B companies. Our mission is to equip founders with the resources, expertise, and connections they need to build and scale successfully. Our portfolio brings together our own incubations, accelerator alumni, and strategic investments. The businesses we oversee have generated $100+ million in cumulative revenue and raised $40M+ in funding. We've built a network of 450+ corporate clients and 100+ mentors and investors across various industries.

We can provide investment and act as an operating partner to accelerate your company's growth. If you believe we can help, please contact us at contact@stakkventures.xyz .
